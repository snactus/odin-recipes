Chicken Al Gool

Description

Chicken Al Gool is a delicous dish from the nether regions of the country. It's a cultural favorite of the people who live in the area of the land that they live in. An abundance of chicken in the 1800's led to farmers getting creative with their cooking, and creating this hearty dish. Packed with protein, nutrients, vitamins, fiber, sugar, spice, everything nice, and nitrogen based industrial detergents, Chicken Al Gool is a dish that can be made in a day, and last a year. Your family is sure to love this dish.

Ingredients

  • Three whole raw chickens
  • One pound jasmine rice
  • One gallon beef stock
  • One cup salt
  • Five cups brown sugar
  • Two cups cane sugar
  • One Tbsp bull semen
  • One gallon high-fructose corn syrup
  • One soft pretzel
  • Assorted vegetables
  • One jug Tide Heavy Duty Hygenic Detergent
  • Spices to taste

Directions

Part one

Begin preparation of this dish four days before you wish to eat it. Start by removing the feet of your three chickens. Set them aside in a freezer. Leave one of the raw chickens out in a warm, humid place. Using a blender, combine the other two chickens and the jug of detergent and blend into a thick puree. Set your puree in the fridge or freezer.

Part two

Begin the second part of the preparation on the day you wish to eat the meal. Begin by bringing a large pot of water to a roiling boil. Add the brown sugar and stir until it dissolves. Add the jasmine rice to the thick sugary liquid. In a second pot combine the high-fructose corn syrup and spices over heat. Add your puree to this mixture and let it cook on low heat. let simmer while you cook. Fill a third pot with the beef stock. Add the assorted vegetables. Add the cane sugar. Add the bull semen. Add the chicken feet you prepared earlier. Let these boil. Take the raw chicken you left on the counter and put it on a large platter. Put the soft pretzel on the top of the rotten chicken. The rice should have turned into a sort of mush now. Pour the rice over the top of the chicken and pretzel. Then pour over your sweet chicken puree. Then add your sweet veggie-beef-semen concoction to the top. Garnish with the chicken feet. Pour your cup of salt over the top of the dish and serve.
